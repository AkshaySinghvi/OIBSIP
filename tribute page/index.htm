Steve Wozniak

1950 - Present

______________________________________________

Steve Wozniak, also known as "Woz," is an American inventor, electronics engineer, and computer programmer who co-founded Apple Inc. with Steve Jobs and Ronald Wayne.

_______________________________________________

Biography:

Steve Wozniak was born on August 11, 1950, in San Jose, California. He developed an interest in electronics from a young age and became involved in computer design and programming. In 1976, he and Steve Jobs created the Apple I, a single-board computer that laid the foundation for Apple Inc.

Wozniak's technical skills were pivotal in the success of Apple's early products. He designed the hardware for the Apple I and Apple II computers, which became highly popular in the personal computer industry.

Throughout his career, Wozniak has been recognized for his contributions to the tech industry, receiving numerous awards and honors.
